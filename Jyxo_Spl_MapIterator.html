Class Jyxo_Spl_MapIterator

Iterator which applies a callback over results (lazy-loaded calls).
Supports iteration over both Traversable and array.

Jyxo_Spl_MapIterator implements SeekableIterator, OuterIterator, Jyxo_Spl_ArrayCopy, Countable
